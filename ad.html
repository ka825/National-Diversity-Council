Diversity is a fact Inclusion is an act

Inclusion comprehends people’s involvement and empowerment. When people are included, they recognize their worth and feel respected, which helps them be their own authentic selves.

Disability Inclusion

Disability inclusion is understanding the way people function and participate in society. Assuring that everybody has the same opportunities in every aspect of life.

Age Diversity

The definition of age diversity is the acceptance of people of different ages, especially in the workplace. Age diversity is like generational diversity and seeks to combat age discrimination.

Religious diversity

Religious diversity is accepting the fact that there are significant differences in religious beliefs and practices, and embracing different religions would be beneficial for people.
